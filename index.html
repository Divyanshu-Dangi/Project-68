Gurukul Kurushetra

Mission Statement Founded in 1912 by Swami Shardhanand Ji with grand vision of inculcating Indian ethos and scientific temperament in the young minds, Gurukul Kurukshetra has been on mission mode ever since its inception to provide public school education from its sprawling 40 Acres campus to create safe, secure, happy and stimulating learning environment to instill honor, respect and compassion in each student and prepare him for success throughout his life.

Theoretical knowledge of Computer Science alone can’t make our young generation substantial enough to keep growing in today’s world. However they need to understand and get practical knowledge at a broader pace. Gurukul Kurukshetra is packed with Hi-Tech Computer Labs which offer the students to gain hands on experience of the modern Information Technology and acquire the practical knowledge and the applications of modern computer science. Gurukul Kurukshetra offers two separate computer labs for the students and teachers. Computer Lab –I: This lab provides fully interactive environment for the juniors to learn the basic operation and applications of computers. This lab having a capacity of 25 computers is connected to a powerful server. The lab is designed for computer aided learning, equipped with L.C.D projectors and multi-media software. Computer Lab –II: Offers all the resources and equipments for advanced level programming and developing high end animations and client-server applications for the senior students. This lab is also used by the teachers for finding latest information from Internet which makes their teaching aids and methodology up to date and globally at par. This lab is well maintained and well equipped with Wi-Fi internet facility and the latest computers to provide hands on experience on computer know how. This very spacious and air conditioned lab is having a capacity of 55 separate computers, one L.C.D projector and one online UPS for smooth operation of each computer with 24x7 full power backup.
